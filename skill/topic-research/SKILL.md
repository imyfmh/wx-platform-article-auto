# Topic Research

## Workflow

1. Clarify topic scope, region, and timeframe.
2. Collect 5–12 high-quality sources (prefer primary sources).
3. Extract key points, numbers, dates, and opposing views.
4. Mark each point with confidence: high/medium/low.
5. Produce a research brief for drafting.
